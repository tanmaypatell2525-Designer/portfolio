02 / About

I like making things

I’m Tanmay, a product designer focused on turning ambitious ideas into products people genuinely enjoy using.

My strength as a Product Designer is understanding user needs through user research, identifying pain points, and designing solutions that solve real user problems and create meaningful experiences.

I care about the full journey, from the first sketch to the final interaction. My work combines strategic thinking, visual craft, and thoughtful technology.

When I’m not building, I’m usually learning something new, collecting references, or making a needlessly precise cup of coffee.
